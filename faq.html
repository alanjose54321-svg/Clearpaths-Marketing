STRATEGIC SUPPORT

What is your methodology for hospital donor acquisition?
We utilize a high-standard advocacy model focused on monthly sustainer donor recruitment. Our field agents are trained in brand guardianship to ensure your hospital foundation's reputation is protected while we secure long-term revenue.
How do you ensure donor retention and quality?
Quality in face-to-face acquisition is our primary metric. We focus on meaningful conversations rather than volume, resulting in donors who have a higher affinity for your mission and a longer lifetime value (LTV).
Is your agency compliant with Canadian data regulations?
Yes. We strictly adhere to PIPA and PIPEDA regulations. Our acquisition architecture is built with professional-grade data security protocols to ensure all financial transitions are secure and compliant.
How can charities track campaign performance?
Every partner is provided with an executive reporting dashboard. This provides real-time transparency into field activity, donor demographics, and capital acquisition metrics.
