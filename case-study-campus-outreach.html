Case study — Campus Outreach

Recruiting volunteers for a summer program through targeted campus activations and doorstep conversations nearby.

The brief

A youth services organisation needed reliable volunteers for a summer program and wanted measurable signups with onboarding details captured accurately.

Our approach

  • Ran campus activations and targeted door-to-door in adjacent neighbourhoods
  • Used sign-up forms optimized for mobile follow-up and automated data entry
  • Provided on-site supervisors to ensure quality and timely follow-up

Outcome

  • 150 volunteers recruited; 70% remained active after 3 months (example)
  • Clean contact data imported directly to client CRM
