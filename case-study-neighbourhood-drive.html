Case study — Neighbourhood Drive

How a focused door-to-door drive delivered predictable recurring donors.

The brief

A community charity needed recurring donors ahead of winter. They wanted predictable acquisition and clean data for follow-up.

Our approach

  • Selected 12 high-potential pockets from donation and demographic data
  • Trained 12 canvassers with two scripts and tested messaging on small samples
  • Scaled the higher-performing script and supervised teams daily

Outcome

  • 320 new recurring donors in 6 weeks (example)
  • Average first‑month donation: $25
  • Campaign CPA: $36
  • Retention forecast at 12 months: 58%
