Brand Ambassador — Field Representative

Apply now — commissioning paid training and shifts available

Role summary

Represent partner organisations at events and community touchpoints. Run short, respectful conversations and collect opt-in donor data where appropriate.

Responsibilities

  • Deliver clear, on-brand conversations with members of the public
  • Record responses and collect data accurately
  • Follow compliance and safety procedures
  • Attend training and team briefings

Shifts & pay

Field shifts typically run in the afternoon/evening. Example shift window: 12:30 PM – 8:30 PM (actual shifts set per campaign).

How to apply

Email your CV and a short note about why you'd like to work with us to marketingclearpaths@gmail.com. Shortlisted candidates are invited to a group training session.
