Privacy & data handling

We collect personal data only with explicit consent and use it solely for the campaign purposes agreed with the client. Data is stored securely during the campaign and provided to the client in agreed formats (CSV/CRM import) for their ongoing use.

Data minimisation

We limit collection to what is required — name, contact, consent, and gift details where applicable. We do not sell supporter data.

Retention & deletion

Data is transferred to the client on campaign completion. We will delete or transfer data earlier on request as stipulated in the campaign brief.

Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy legislation and follow industry best practices for consent and safeguarding.
